India's Best Leaders

A.P. J. Abdul Kalam

-Former President of India, renowned scientist, and "Missile Man of India."

Dr. B.R. Ambedkar

-Architect of the Indian Constitution, social reformer, and economist.

Mahatma Gandhi

– Leader of India’s independence movement, advocate of nonviolence.

Jawaharlal Nehru

– First Prime Minister of India, key figure in Indian politics pre- and post-independence.

Subhash Chandra Bose

– Freedom fighter, founder of the Indian National Army (INA)

Bhagat Singh

- Revolutionary freedom fighter known for his bravery and martyrdom.

Swami Vivekananda

– Spiritual leader, introduced Indian philosophies to the Western world.
